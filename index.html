What is CR2 to JPG Converter?

Understanding CR2 Files

CR2 (Canon Raw version 2) is Canon's proprietary raw image format that contains unprocessed sensor data from Canon cameras. These files preserve maximum image quality and editing flexibility but are large in size and require specialized software to view and edit.

Why Convert to JPG?

JPG (JPEG) is a universal image format that's widely supported across all devices and platforms. Converting CR2 to JPG makes your images instantly viewable, shareable, and web-ready while significantly reducing file size.
